# Yanka Agri Instagram Promotional Flyer 🌱

## Project Overview
This project contains the design of a promotional Instagram flyer created for **Yanka Agri**, an agricultural initiative focused on empowering farmers, increasing food production, and creating employment opportunities across Africa.

The flyer highlights the core services offered by Yanka Agri and communicates the brand’s mission of supporting modern, sustainable agriculture.

The design was created using **Adobe Illustrator** and optimized for social media promotion.

---

## Design Objective
The main goal of this design was to create a visually appealing promotional flyer that:

- Communicates Yanka Agri’s mission clearly
- Highlights the organization's services
- Uses agricultural visual elements to reinforce the brand identity
- Is optimized for Instagram promotion
- Maintains a clean and professional layout

---

## Brand Message

**YANKA AGRI**  
*Empowering Agriculture. Feeding the Future.* 🌱

The flyer promotes the organization's commitment to improving agricultural productivity, supporting farmers, and building sustainable food systems.

---

## Services Highlighted in the Flyer

### 🌾 Farmer Training Programs
Training farmers and young people on modern farming techniques, crop management, and sustainable agriculture.

### 🚜 Agricultural Technology Support
Introducing modern farming tools, improved seeds, irrigation systems, and mechanized farming solutions.

### 🌱 Farm Investment & Cooperative Farming
Creating opportunities for individuals and communities to invest in farms while supporting local farmers.

### 🏭 Food Processing & Value Addition
Processing agricultural products such as rice, cassava, maize, and palm produce into market-ready products.

### 🧑‍🌾 Agricultural Supply Network
Providing farmers with fertilizers, seedlings, pesticides, and farming tools at affordable prices.

### 🛒 Market Access for Farmers
Connecting farmers directly to buyers, markets, restaurants, and food companies.

### 🌍 Community Farms & Youth Employment
Developing community farms that create jobs, train young people, and increase food production.

---

## Design Features

- **Maize-inspired “Y” graphic**
  - The letter **Y** was redesigned to resemble a maize (corn) plant, reinforcing the agricultural theme.

- **Agriculture-themed color palette**
  - Dark green background (growth and sustainability)
  - Maize yellow accents (harvest and food production)
  - Light green highlights (nature and renewal)

- **Instagram optimized layout**
  - Canvas size: **1080 × 1350 px**

- **Readable information hierarchy**
  - Bold heading
  - Clear service descriptions
  - Structured sections

---

## Tools Used

- **Adobe Illustrator (v30.2.1)**
- Vector illustration and typography tools
- Custom shape design for the maize-inspired logo element
